## 🚀 Overview
**Classmate Connect** is a specialized networking platform. It automatically categorizes users into specific batches based on their educational background, allowing seamless communication between verified classmates.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### 📡 **Smart Social Feed**
* **Batch-Specific Visibility:** See posts only from your specific institution and year.
* **Rich Interactions:** Real-time **Likes** and **Comments** on every post.
* **Media Support:** Upload images along with text updates.
* **Smart Timestamps:** Relative time formatting (e.g., "Just now", "2h ago").

### 💬 **Secure Private Messaging**
* **Instant Chat:** Real-time peer-to-peer messaging.
* **Image Sharing:** Send photos directly within the chat window.
* **Message Control:** Long-press or click to **Delete for Everyone**.
* **Safety First:** Built-in **User Blocking** and **Post Reporting** systems.

### 🔍 **Advanced Directory Search**
* **Multi-Filter System:** Find classmates using **Institution, City, Class, or Graduation Year**.
* **Quick Connections:** Manage pending requests via a notification-enabled dashboard.

### 🛠️ **System Features**
* **PWA Ready:** Fully installable as a home screen app with a custom icon.
* **Dark Mode:** Support for low-light environments.
* **WhatsApp Invite:** Spread the word to your batch with a single click.

## 📲 Installation Guide
1. Open [Classmate Connect](https://dommatamadhu-a11y.github.io/Classmate-Connect/) in your mobile browser.
2. Tap the **Menu** (three dots) or **Share** icon.
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Launch the app from your home screen for a full-screen experience.
